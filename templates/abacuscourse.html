A perfect tool for the mind

A proven track record through history and recent neuroscientific research has positively confirmed the effectiveness of the abacus training. Calculating on an ancient abacus that utilizes a hands-on, kid-friendly, and multiple intelligence approach has proven benefits to enhance the development of human brain power.

Move fingers and activate nerve cells

  • Nerve cells in the neocortex of the brain consist of 14 billion sets of motor nerves and sensory nerves.
  • Moving fingers and talking aloud lead to activation by providing appropriate stimuli in the large part of sensory-to-motor domains in the cerebral neocortex.
  • In this sense, starting abacus learning as young as possible is useful in activating the brains of young children.

Development of the right brain

How Abacus helps in Brain Development?

As it was said that our brain is divided into two parts, Left and Right brain, our left brain helps in logical calculations, analysing things, mathematical calculation, storing words and our right brain helps in creative thinking, visualizations, imagination and reasoning ability.

Nowadays, education techniques develop the left brain only. But when children practice on abacus, they use their both hands. Abacus gives the exercise to both sides of the brain, and when a child does the mental arithmetic, he concentrates and visualizes the picture of Abacus. With each calculation, he changes the position of beads in his mind instantly. This process is an excellent exercise for the brain and in turn, it helps in brain development.

BrainFast Vedic Maths

Introduction

Vedic Maths is an ancient system of mathematics that originated in India. It consists of techniques and principles for solving mathematical problems in a faster and more efficient manner compared to conventional methods. The system is based on 16 sutras (aphorisms) and 13 sub-sutras (corollaries) that cover various aspects of mathematics, including arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and calculus.

Key Benefits

  • Speed: Enables quicker calculations, often mentally.
  • Accuracy: Reduces the likelihood of errors in complex calculations.
  • Simplicity: Simplifies complex problems into easy steps
  • Flexibility: Offers multiple methods to solve a single problem
  • Confidence: Builds mathematical confidence and reduces math anxiety

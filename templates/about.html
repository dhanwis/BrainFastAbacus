BrainFast Abacus & Vedics


Brainfast Abacus &Vedics is an educational program designed to enhance mathematical skills in students through the use of the abacus, an ancient calculating tool. The program focuses on developing mental arithmetic abilities, improving concentration, and boosting overall cognitive skills. By using the abacus, students learn to visualize numbers and calculations, which helps them perform arithmetic operations quickly and accurately. Brainfast Abacus aims to build a strong foundation in math for students, making the learning process engaging and effective

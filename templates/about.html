BrainFast Abacus & Vedics


Brainfast Abacus &Vedics is an educational program designed to enhance mathematical skills in students through the use of the abacus, an ancient calculating tool. The program focuses on developing mental arithmetic abilities, improving concentration, and boosting overall cognitive skills. By using the abacus, students learn to visualize numbers and calculations, which helps them perform arithmetic operations quickly and accurately. Brainfast Abacus aims to build a strong foundation in math for students, making the learning process engaging and effective

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower young minds with exceptional mathematical skills and cognitive abilities through innovative and engaging abacus training. We are committed to providing a supportive and nurturing learning environment that fosters confidence, concentration, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning. Our goal is to help 10,00,000 students unlock their full potential and achieve academic excellence.

Our Vision

Our vision at Brainfast Abacus & Vedic Maths is to become a globally recognized leader in Abacus and Vedic education, inspiring and transforming the lives of students worldwide. We envision a future where every child has the opportunity to excel in mathematics and develop critical thinking skills that will serve them in all areas of life. Through our dedication to quality education and continuous improvement, we strive to create a positive and lasting impact on the communities we serve.

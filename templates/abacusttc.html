BrainFast Abacus Teacher Training Course

An BrainFast Abacus Teacher Training Course is designed to equip individuals with the necessary skills and knowledge to teach the abacus method effectively to students. The course covers both the theoretical and practical aspects of using the abacus for mental arithmetic. Here's a breakdown of what you can generally expect from such a training program

BrainFast Abacus Teachers Training Course Structure

Typically, an Abacus Teacher Training Course consists of several modules that progressively build your understanding and teaching abilities.

Module Title
Module 1 Introduction to Abacus & Brain Development
Module 2 Basics of Abacus Calculation
Module 3 Advanced Abacus Techniques
Module 4 Teaching Methodologies
Module 5 Classroom Management & Student Engagement
Module 6 Practical Training

Job Opportunities

  • Private Tutoring: Offer abacus classes for students of various age groups.
  • Abacus Centers: Join existing learning centers or coaching institutes.
  • Online Teaching: Teach online through established platforms or by setting up your own courses.
  • Start Your Own Abacus Academy: Offers multiple methods to solve a single problem.

Benefits of Learning Abacus Teaching

Increased Employment Opportunities

Certified abacus teachers are in demand globally due to the increasing interest in mental math education.

Intellectual Growth

Abacus teaching enhances your mathematical, cognitive, and problem-solving skills.

Helping Others

You'll be able to impart valuable skills to young learners, aiding in their academic development.

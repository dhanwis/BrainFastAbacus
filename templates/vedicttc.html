BrainFast Vedic Maths Teacher Training Course

The Brainfast Vedic Maths Teachers Training Course is designed to equip educators with the knowledge and skills needed to teach Vedic Mathematics effectively. Vedic Mathematics is a system of mathematics that simplifies complex calculations and enhances mental agility. The course typically includes the following:

Key Features of the Course:

  • Introduction to Vedic Mathematics: : Understanding the history and principles of Vedic Mathematics, which is based on ancient Indian texts.
  • Techniques and Sutras: : Learning the 16 sutras (formulas) and 13 sub-sutras (corollaries) that form the basis of Vedic Maths, helping teachers explain and demonstrate shortcuts for addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and more.
  • Mental Calculation Methods: Training teachers to guide students in performing quick mental arithmetic, boosting their confidence and speed in solving mathematical problems.
  • Practical Applications: Using Vedic Maths in real-life problem-solving scenarios, not just for theoretical understanding but also for day-to-day tasks.
  • Structured Lesson Plans: Providing a step-by-step framework for teachers to create engaging and effective lessons for students of different age groups and skill levels.
  • Student Engagement: Techniques to keep students interested and motivated through games, activities, and challenges that utilize Vedic Math methods.
  • Course Materials: Access to resources like textbooks, worksheets, and practice tests to help both the teacher and students.
